My favourite Movies and their Reviews

My top recommendations of all time

Orange

Orange is a pure love story of Ram.
He has 10 love stories among which 1 is for 1 year and remaining is for 3 years.
But, finally he finds a genuine love in his life and he understands what the real love actually is.

Chandramukhi

It is a horror story of a dancer Chandramukhi who was killed by a king.
The story is all about how she takes revenge on that mercyless king.

Awara

This is a story of a person who meets a girl by mistake and travells along with her to reach her hometown.
The movie is starring Karthi and Thamanna as main leads
